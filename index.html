Brandon Frazier

After I graduated high school, I decided to join the Army. In the Army I went from Kentucky to Germany during my enlistment. I was a scout assigned to the 1st Batallion 4th Infantry Regiment, stationed in Hohnefels, Germany. When my enlistment was over, I went home and enrolled at Arizona State University. I graduated with a B.A. in English in 2001. Degree in hand, I decided to teach English in Budapest, Hungary, and later, Prague, The Czech Republic. after about a year of teaching, I returned stateside, and decided to return to school to earn my Master Degree. After I earned my degree, I began work towards my licensure as a Marriage, Family, and Child Therapist. While guiding others through their cognitive process, I realized that my true passion was in the process. By Process, I mean, more specifically, the cognitive process of human beahvior. Essentially, the nuts and bolts of what we do and our motivations for doing what we do. Seeking a better understanding of the process naturally led me to technology due to the vast capabilities and untapped potential.
